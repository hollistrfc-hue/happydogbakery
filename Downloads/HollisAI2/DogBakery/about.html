Who We Are

Born Right Here in Stillwater

Happy Dog Barkery & Boutique was built from a love of dogs and a belief that Stillwater deserved something special — a real, community-rooted shop where tails wag and owners feel at home.

We bake every treat in-store, from scratch, with ingredients we're proud of. No mystery fillers, no warehouse shipping — just fresh baked from our kitchen to your pup's bowl.

We stock our boutique with products we'd use on our own dogs: quality chews, nutritious food, durable toys, and accessories that last. And when it comes to grooming and self-wash, we show up with the supplies so you can focus on the fun part.

We're your neighbors. We're in Stillwater because we love Stillwater — and we're honored to be part of your dog's story.
